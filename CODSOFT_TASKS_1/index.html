The Story

Started in a garage.
Still made like one.

Ember & Ash began in 2019 with three peppers, a stockpot, and a landlord who was not thrilled about the smell. We haven't changed much since — we still ferment every mash by hand, still taste every batch before it's bottled, and still refuse to tell you a sauce is mild when it isn't.

No fillers. No shortcuts. No sauce leaves the kitchen until it earns its label.

How to Use

Start lower than you think.

Taste every new bottle on its own before it touches food — a drop on a spoon tells you more than a whole plate ever will. Heat builds the longer it sits, so give it thirty seconds before reaching for the water.
